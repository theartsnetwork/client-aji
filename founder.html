Founder's Message

Our Founder

Ajinkya Roadways started by my father grown many folds since late 1970s while focusing on core principle of 'delivering the best with a personal touch’. We are known in the market for on-time delivery and highest standards.

Our past and existing clients love us and continued to work throughout years with us. I feel blessed to have their thrust and most importantly, I’d never forget the early angels who helped me in the beginning when we were a small startup without any track record.

With changing times, my sons are helping me to take the firm to the next level with new technologies, larger network, multimodal fleet and more effective operations. We would continue to serve our clients for years to come and give them the best experience possible.

Yours sincerely
